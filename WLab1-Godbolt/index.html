Covettvete C8 z06

The Chevrolet Corvette C8 Z06 is a high-performance variant of the eighth-generation Corvette, officially unveiled on October 26, 2021, as a 2023 model year vehicle1. This model is designed to push the boundaries of track capability and performance.

History

The ‘next generation’ Corvette debuts in Tustin, California on July 18, 2019 and is the brand’s first-ever production mid-engine Corvette. The Z06 designation first appeared in 1963 as an options package for the Corvette C2. Developed by Zora Arkus-Duntov, the “father of the Corvette,” the package was designed for racing enthusiasts. It included a 360-horsepower fuel-injected V8 engine, heavy-duty suspension, and larger brakes12. Only 199 units were produced, making it a rare and sought-after model

Car Specs

  • Engine: 5.5-liter V8 with a flat-plane crankshaft
  • Horsepower: 670 hp at 8,400 rpm
  • Torque: 460 lb-ft at 6,300 rpm
  • Redline: 8,600 rpm
  • 0-60 mph: 2.6 seconds
  • Top Speed: 195 mph
  • Transmission: 8-speed dual-clutch
  • Drive Layout: Mid-engine, rear-wheel drive
